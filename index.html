the “photographer virus” to me. From analogue to digital. I have taken
a number of courses and workshops and, above all, practiced a lot! My
children are now used to modeling. Perhaps that is where the penchant
for taking photos of children comes from. The pure appearance and
enthusiasm of children. It's always fun!

feelings within a frame.”
